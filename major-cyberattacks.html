Major Russian Cyberattacks

2007 – Estonia DDoS Attacks

Massive DDoS attacks disrupted Estonia’s government and banking systems, marking the first cyberwarfare incident.

2016 – DNC Hack

Russian hackers breached the DNC, leaking emails that influenced the U.S. election.

2017 – NotPetya Malware

NotPetya malware spread globally, causing $10B+ in damages.

2020 – SolarWinds Attack

Russian hackers infiltrated SolarWinds software, affecting 18,000 organizations worldwide.
